Diseases

Plant Diseases:

1. Powdery Mildew:

Leaves, stems, and flowers develop a white dusty coating

2. Downy Mildew:

Upper portion of leaves discolor, while the bottoms develop white or gray mold

3. Black Spot:

Small black spots appear on foliage, which turns yellow and drops off

4. Mosaic Virus:

Causes mottled or distorted leaves

5. Root Rot:

Roots become mushy and black due to overwatering or poor drainage

Crop Diseases:

1. Bacterial Blight:

Causes wilting and yellowing of leaves.

2. Fusarium Wilt:

Leads to yellowing and wilting of plants.

3. Clubroot:

Roots become swollen and distorted6.

4. Late Blight:

Causes dark spots on leaves and stems, leading to plant decay6.

5. Anthracnose:

Causes dark, sunken spots on fruits and leaves.

Vegetable Diseases:

1. Damping Off:

Seedlings rot at the base and collapse.

2. Leaf Spot:

Circular spots appear on leaves.

3. Powdery Mildew:

White powdery spots on leaves.

4. Root Knot Nematode:

Roots develop knots or galls.

5. Bacterial Soft Rot:

Soft, mushy areas develop on vegetables.
