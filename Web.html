About Project

Details

"The Agricultural Crop Recommendation System using machine learning is a software application that utilizes artificial intelligence algorithms to provide recommendations to farmers on the best crops to plant based on their specific farming conditions. The system collects and analyzes various data sources, such as soil quality, climate, and historical crop yields, to make personalized crop recommendations. The system is easy to use and can be accessed through a web-based platform or mobile app."
